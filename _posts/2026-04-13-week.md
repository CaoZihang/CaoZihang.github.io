## [cloc丨Count Lines of Code](https://github.com/AlDanial/cloc)

- file & directory & archive & git repo: `cloc [path]`
- Iterative `for d in ./*/ ; do (cd "$d" && echo "$d" && cloc --vcs git); done`
- help `cloc --help`

## [Tree-Sitter](https://tree-sitter.github.io/tree-sitter/)]
